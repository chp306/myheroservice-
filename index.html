FAQ

Got questions? Honest answers.

Do I need to be on social media already?
No. We can work with a brand new account or an existing one. We'll assess where you are and build from there.
Will the content actually sound like me?
Yes — that's the whole point. We build a brand voice document from your onboarding call, and every piece of content is written to match your tone. If anything doesn't sound right, we revise it for free until it does.
I've tried other services and got generic content. How is this different?
Most services use templates. We don't. Your content is created from scratch using AI research specific to your brand, your niche, and your audience. It's built to sell your products — not just fill a grid.
What do I actually have to do?
Show up for a 30-minute onboarding call. After that, review your content pack and approve it. That's it. We handle everything else.
Do you post the content for me?
On the Starter plan, we deliver everything and you post it yourself. On Growth and Scale plans, we handle scheduling too — so you genuinely don't have to touch it.
Is this just AI writing everything automatically?
AI powers the research and drafting — but every piece of content is reviewed, refined, and quality-checked before it reaches you. You get the speed of AI with the judgement of a real strategist.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contracts, no minimum term. Monthly rolling, cancel with 30 days notice. We earn your business every single month.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see engagement improvements within 2–4 weeks of consistent posting. Follower growth typically kicks in from month 2 as the algorithm picks up your consistency and starts pushing your content to new audiences.
