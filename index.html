The process

Live in 48 hours.
Here's exactly how.

A simple 4-step process that runs itself every single month.

Step 1
Day 1 — 30 minutes

Onboarding call

Before your call, fill in a short form so we can hit the ground running. The call itself takes 30 minutes — we learn your brand, products, tone, and goals. That's all we need.

Step 2
Day 1–2 — We get to work

AI research & content creation

We run AI-powered audience research, build your brand voice document, write 30 custom captions, build your content calendar, and package your Reel ideas — all within 48 hours of your call.

Step 3
Day 2 — In your inbox

You review and approve

We send your full content pack as a Google Doc. Review it, request any tweaks, and approve. Revisions are unlimited — we turn them around fast. Most clients approve within 24 hours.

Step 4
Every month — Ongoing

We analyse, improve, repeat

Each month we check your analytics, identify what's performing, and use those insights to make the next month's content even sharper. The strategy keeps improving the longer you work with us.
